Libin Institute Research Symposium
The Libin Cardiovascular Institute Trainee Organization & O’Brien Institute Postdoctoral Scholars Association are collaboratively hosting a research symposium for undergraduate summer students from Libin/O’Brien on August 14, 2023.
Any undergraduate summer student whose supervisor is affiliated with either O’Brien or Libin (so this should include many of the CHI faculty members) is eligible to submit an abstract for presentation. We would welcome the re-presentation/re-use of abstracts/posters already submitted to other summer student research day events or conferences. There will be prizes awarded for top presentations.

Learn R Shiny Together
Join our self-learning group to become an R Shiny expert. No teachers, just a supportive group of enthusiasts exploring interactive data visualization.
