New Leave Request
On October 10, a new online Leave Request Form launched replacing the Time off Request form. The new form is specifically for MaPS and Support Staff (AUPE) to initiate certain leaves, including Maternity, Parental, Leave without Pay and other Employment Standards leaves. Employees can access the new form via Employee Self-Service through the My UCalgary portal (navigate to All about Me > My Info > Self Service > HR Forms).

The leave form will automatically route to managers and HR for approval and once approved, HR will process the leave following existing processes. For more information on the Leave Request Form, visit the HR Website.
