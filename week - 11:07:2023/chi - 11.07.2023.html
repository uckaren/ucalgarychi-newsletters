cold & flu season

As cold and flu season begins, I’d like to remind staff that if you are sick or have been around someone who is sick, please stay home. If you are well enough, you can work from home but if you are symptomatic and unwell, please inform your supervisor and take a sick day. All staff and students are encouraged to book appointments to get their flu vaccine and Covid-19 booster this fall, if you haven’t already done so.

Let’s keep everyone as safe and healthy as possible!

standards specialist

Canada Health Infoway in collaboration with Federal, Provincial and Territorial Governments has recently developed and published a Shared pan-Canadian Interoperability Roadmap. This Roadmap identifies a number of standards-based ecosystem interoperability products that will require development in partnership with the F/P/T and other industry partners. The National Release Center (NRC) is the single point of distribution of the baseline Health Terminologies and Exchange Standards used in Canada.

Infoway is looking for an experienced Standards Specialist to drive and support the NRC standards development activities associated with the interoperability roadmap, the Infoway Products as well as the pan-Canadian Standards implementations. To learn more or to apply visit here
