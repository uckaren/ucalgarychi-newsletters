where do the office supplies belong?

If you aren’t sure where office supplies belong like extension cords, HDMI cables, extra keyboards and mice, monitors and monitor boxes, etc. Please see below or ask myself, Karen or Rae if you aren’t sure where to put something! Most things belong either in the locked storage room by the bathrooms (Karen, Rae, and Sarah have keys) or in the island filing cabinet by the cubicles. It is everyone’s responsibility to put things back where they belong or find storage for their items. Please do not leave items on someone’s desk to put away for you.

Office Supplies: e.g., HDMI cables, extension cords, power bars etc. go in the island storage cabinet. There are several drawers facing Shabnam’s office where these live.

Keyboards, Mice, Monitors: any extras can be put in the storage room. Please connect with Karen, Sarah, or Rae so they can unlock it for you.

Poster Tubes: personal ones can be stored at your own desk or extras to share can be put in the storage room.

Monitor Boxes:you are responsible for disposal or storage of boxes any of your equipment belongs in. If there is room in the storage room, they may be placed in there otherwise please store at your own desk/office.

Any questions, please feel free to ask Erin.
