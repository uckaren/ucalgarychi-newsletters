We are filming a Video Promotion to Highlight CHI!
Happy Friday Everyone!
I’m writing to you today to let you know that the office of VP Advancement and the Cumming School of Medicine Team will be coming to the Centre on March 23rd, 2023 from 8am-2pm to film a video promotion for CHI!
On behalf of our Executive Leadership, I want to congratulate everyone on their hard work. It’s because of the success we have had as a team that we have been given this opportunity by the University of Calgary.
We realize that this is a tight turnaround, but we encourage everyone to come into the office on March 23rd, 2023. We would love to show off our team! If you do come into the office, please be aware of the following:
1. There will be no lunchtime ping pong. As we there will be a professional film crew coming to work, we want to free up the space for them to use as they see fit.
2. Please expect a lot of noise and interruptions- we may grab you to be in background shots or b-roll. If you have a lot of meetings or need to concentrate on March 23rd, we encourage you to switch your in-office days and work remotely.
3. Meeting rooms (ie. Quantum, Turing, Gosling) will be booked off and will not be available from 8am -2pm.If you currently have a room booking, I will be reaching out shortly to make alternate arrangements.
4. If you are camera shy and would prefer to not be on film but would still like to participate, please let me know ahead of time. The final product is estimated to be between 1:30-2:00 minutes long. We want to highlight as much of CHI and our team, but there’s no guarantee that everything will make the final edit.
5. We will be offering lunch on this day. Please let me know if you have any dietary restrictions as soon as Tuesday March 21st, 2023.
I will be sending out an Outlook Calendar invite shortly so you can block this time out on your schedules. Please RVSP to the calendar invite if you will be in the office on Thursday, so we know how many staff to expect. Thank you all for your consideration! Feel free to reach out to me should you have any questions or concerns.
