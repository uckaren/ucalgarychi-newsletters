ahs is hiring a senior analyst, biostatistics

The Senior Analyst -Biostatistics uses multiple and diverse datasets including population, socio-demographics, Stats Canada and health care utilization data to help the team describe the health of populations across the province, seek to uncover and understand variation, and to assess the impact of public health interventions on population health and its variation.

This position is highly proficient in organizing and manipulating data and creating new datasets from existing data sources for analysis and reporting using a variety of sophisticated tools and approaches including accessing large datasets on Oracle data servers, statistical software coding in SAS or R, statistical modeling in SAS or R or other statistical software, and use of GIS and interactive web reporting tools such as Tableau.

To learn more or to apply, please visit AHS Hiring Board. AHS Hiring Board. Application due date is November 30th, 2023.

Prescribing Large Language Models
for Medicine: What’s The Right Dose?
What are the most impactful applications of AI – and Large Language Models (LLMs) in particular – for clinical decision-making and administrative tasks in health care? Although LLMs have existed for several years, it is only with the widespread availability of tools like ChatGPT that many possible health care applications have come to light. Yet, the question remains: How can we ensure their quality, safety, and efficacy?

In this free virtual event, editors from the forthcoming journal NEJM AI will speak with industry experts about the potential of LLMs to transform health care, exploring both the benefits and dangers of current and future applications.

Thursday, September 14th, 2023
3pm – 5pm MST
