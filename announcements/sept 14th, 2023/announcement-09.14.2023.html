As we head into the Fall, we have some exciting changes in CHI to announce that came into effect September 1! CHI Operations Manager, Dr. Cathy Eastwood, transitioning to a newly created role as Associate Director, Research and Strategic Partnerships and Operations and Development Specialist, Erin O’Connor, moved to the role of CHI Business and Operations Manager.
Cathy will be focusing on leading CHI’s research activity and building CHI’s internal and external partnerships. Erin will be responsible for the day-to-day operations of CHI, finances, and the research services team. Cathy and Erin have been integral parts of growing and running CHI since 2019 and we are excited to see them grow in their new roles. HR reports-to changes related to their new roles will be communicated to staff over the next little while and we aim to make this transition as seamless as possible. Congratulations Cathy and Erin!
Also, as of September 1, 2023, we have welcomed new faculty member Dr. Jessalyn Holodinksy. Dr. Holodinsky is an Assistant Professor in the Departments of Emergency Medicine and Community Health Sciences. She specializes in Biostats and Machine Learning work and will be focusing on Emergency Medicine related projects. Please join me in welcoming Jessalyn!
Last, but not least, I am excited to share that Dr. Colin Josephson has accepted the role of Associate Director, Faculty Engagement and will be focusing on helping CHI build relationships with CSM faculty members and non-faculty clinicians. Colin brings many years of experience in applying data science techniques to the field of epilepsy.
This is just the beginning of the exciting things ahead for us. I look forward to celebrating our accomplishments with you tomorrow as we have our open house. Thank you for your commitment to shared goals. I would say that we made some massive steps forward in becoming the trusted resource we aspire to be.
-Tyler
