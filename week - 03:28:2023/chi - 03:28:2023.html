Principles of Biostatistics - Online Course

Do you want to improve your data analysis skills, but struggle to find the time for a traditional biostatistics course?

The fast-track online biostatistics course is designed for you! Section I of this training will cover fundamental principles and practical tools for statistical analysis in healthcare and research. Section II will focus on data analysis using R software, ideal for managing and analyzing large and complex datasets.

Gain the essential skills you need to succeed in your career! Courses can be taken separately or together.
