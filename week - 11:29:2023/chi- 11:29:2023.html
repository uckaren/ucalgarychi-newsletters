Invitation for CAHSPR Abstract Reviewers!
The Canadian Association for Health Services and Policy Research invites members of the CAHSR community to consider supporting the organization by applying to be a reviewer for the call for abstracts for the 2024 Annual Conference.

Each year, members of the health services and policy research community submit an overwhelming number of abstracts in hopes of being selected to present their work at the Annual CAHSPR Conference. This process requires the submitters’ work to be reviewed by their peers with expertise in their abstract’s theme.

If you are interested in being considered as a reviewer, and you are able to accommodate the timelines below, we invite you to click the link below to submit your application to become an abstract reviewer.
